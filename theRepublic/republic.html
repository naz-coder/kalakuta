Yes, guys, Afro beats legend, Fela Anikulapo Kuti got married to 27 women in one day back in 1978 in what was seen as a very daring move. According to reports, Fela decided to make the bold move after rumours began to filter the air that he was holding the women in his band as captives

A proponent of polygamy, he married 27 women at the same time in 1978. He then switched to a rotating system of having 12 wives at a time, but eventually came to think of marriage as "imposition" and he divorced all of them. His attitude towards women (in his music and personal life) has come under scrutiny and accusations of misogyny have been directed at him.
